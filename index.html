What is CodingClash?

CodingClash is a virtual strategy hackathon that spans 2 weeks. In this time, contestants work together in teams to build AI bots that dictate how troops in the game move. These teams will be able to implement a broad variety of algorithms including bug navigation, A* searches, neural networks, and much more!

Contestants are given a starter scaffold with a basic bot example. The game engine and visualizer will also be provided so that teams can test their code locally. To play against other teams, gain elo points, and be considered for tournaments, the team must upload its bot to the competition website.

What languages are supported?

CodingClash will support Python 3.8 in 2020! We did this so that our competition can be as accessible as possible, without the use of more scary programming languages.
